Managing (with) Intelligent Algorithms: Summer Workshop

This virtual workshop brings together scholars and practitioners interested in advancing our understanding of how intelligent algorithms shift the nature of work, organizing, and strategy. This is a place to discuss new trends observed in practice and the new research making sense of those trends. Tell us what you’re reading and what excites you about new organizational uses of intelligent algorithms. Scholars in all career stages and from all areas of study are invited to participate.
