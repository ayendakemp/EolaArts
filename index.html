Eola AI

Eola AI is a co-learning community and organizational research lab at the intersection of business and technology. We conduct original research to examine how intelligent technologies impact organizations and markets.

We focus on understanding how both entrepreneurs and existing organizations can leverage technologies such as artificial intelligence and blockchain to create and capture value for their primary stakeholders.
