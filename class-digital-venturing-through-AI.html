Venturing with Artificial Intelligence

The course asks students to identify and analyze a market opportunity that uses artificial intelligence in its core capabilities. Here, artifiicartificialal intelligence must constitute a core part to the value proposition stakeholders. Each module of the course represents a stage in the strategizing process. But the modules may be implemented iteratively through unique sequences, as each project demands. The instructor guides students as they iterate through the seven modules. The final deliverable for the course is a business plan and (or) a business pitch explaining the outcomes of the analysis.
